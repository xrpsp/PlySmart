About the Game

Persona 5: The Phantom X is a new mobile RPG set in the Persona universe. Experience the life of a high school student who becomes a Phantom Thief to change the hearts of corrupt adults. With new characters, personas, and storylines, this game brings the Persona 5 experience to mobile devices.

Key Features

  • Engaging turn-based combat system
  • Deep social simulation elements
  • Stylish UI and visuals true to the Persona series
  • New characters and storylines
  • Persona fusion and collection
  • Day/night cycle affecting gameplay

System Requirements

Android: Android 9.0 or higher, 4GB RAM, Snapdragon 845/equivalent or better

iOS: iOS 13.0 or later, iPhone 8 or newer, iPad Air (3rd gen) or newer

Storage: 3.2 GB available space

Story

You are a high school student in Tokyo who discovers the power of Persona. Along with your friends, you form the Phantom Thieves to enter the Metaverse and change the hearts of corrupt adults. Balance your daily school life with your secret phantom thief activities.
